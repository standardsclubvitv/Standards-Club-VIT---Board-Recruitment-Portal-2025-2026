Rules & Regulations

Important information before you apply

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a current student of VIT Vellore
  • Valid VIT email ID (@vitstudent.ac.in) required
  • No current disciplinary actions or academic probation
  • Commitment to serve full academic year 2025-2026
  • Good academic standing (minimum 7.5 CGPA recommended)

Selection Process

  • Step 1: Online application submission
  • Step 2: Application screening and shortlisting
  • Step 3: First round interview (domain knowledge)
  • Step 4: Final round interview (leadership & vision)
  • Step 5: Final selection and announcement

Important Dates

  • Applications Open: January 15, 2025
  • Application Deadline: February 5, 2025 (11:59 PM)
  • Shortlist Announcement: February 10, 2025
  • Interviews: February 12-15, 2025
  • Final Results: February 20, 2025

Terms & Conditions

  • Each applicant can apply for maximum 3 positions
  • All information must be accurate and verifiable
  • Only one application per email address allowed
  • Resume/Portfolio link must be publicly accessible
  • Shortlisted candidates must attend interviews in person
  • Final decision by selection committee is binding

Pro Tip

Carefully read position descriptions and tailor your responses to demonstrate your fit for each role. Quality over quantity!
